My Love for You

My sweetest love, this heart grows bigger every day because of you—every thought, every moment, filled with how much I adore you.

You’re my everything—my sunrise after the longest night, my safe haven in a chaotic world, my reason to believe in forever. I’d walk through fire, swim through storms, and rewrite the stars just to keep you smiling.

Every moment with you feels like a gift I don’t deserve, yet I’ll spend my life trying to be worthy of.
